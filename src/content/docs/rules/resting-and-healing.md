---
title: Resting And Healing
---

[Guard](/rules/guard) is restored after resting for a couple of minutes.

[Fatigue](/rules/fatigue) clears after a full night's rest & a meal.

[Ability Scores](/rules/ability-scores) recover with a week of bed rest & the
attention of a skilled herbalist.

[Wounds](/rules/fighting/wounds/) have unique recovery conditions.
