---
title: Exposure
---

Characters are [Deprived](/rules/deprivation) when first exposed to cold.  
Then it depends on _how_ cold you are:

|           temperature | interval                              |
| --------------------: | ------------------------------------- |
|      Cold (0º to 10º) | 1d6 Grace damage every **4 hours**    |
| Freezing (-10º to 0º) | 1d6 Grace damage every **1 hour**     |
|   Deadly (below -10º) | 1d6 Grace damage every **10 minutes** |

- At [0 Grace](/rules/ability-scores) you can no longer move; unless rescued,
  you'll die in 1d8 hours.
- You are 10º **warmer** with a
  [winter cloak](/character-creation/gear-tables/#expeditionary-gear), a fire,
  shelter, when fighting.
- You are 10º **colder** when wet, undressed, in wind/rain.
