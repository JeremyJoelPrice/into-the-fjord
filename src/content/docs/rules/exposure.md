---
title: Exposure
---

Characters are [Deprived](/rules/deprivation) when first exposed to cold.  
Then it depends exactly _how_ cold:

|           temperature | interval                            |
| --------------------: | ----------------------------------- |
|      Cold (0º to 10º) | 1d6 Grace damage every **3 hours**  |
| Freezing (-10º to 0º) | 1d6 Grace damage every **1 hour**   |
|   Deadly (below -10º) | 1d6 Grace damage every **1 minute** |

- At [0 Grace](/rules/ability-scores) you can no longer move; unless rescued,
  you'll die in 1d8 hours.
- If you have a winter cloak, you are 10º **warmer**.
- If you're wet or undressed, you are 10º **colder**.
